Our Story

Crafted with
Purpose & Precision

Urban Box Living was born from a vision to make quality, stylish spaces accessible to every South African. We transform industrial shipping containers into fully furnished homes, office spaces, and recreational units — blending modern design with sustainable construction.

From compact 20ft studios to modular office suites and lifestyle builds, every unit is crafted to the highest standard, reflecting our commitment to performance, comfort, and durability.
