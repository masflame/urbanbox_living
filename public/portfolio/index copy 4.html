Our Story

Crafted with
Purpose & Precision

Urban Box Living was born from a vision to make quality, stylish living accessible to every South African. We transform industrial shipping containers into stunning, fully furnished homes — blending modern design with sustainable construction.

From compact 20ft studios to spacious multi-room family homes, every unit is built to the highest standard, reflecting our commitment to craftsmanship, comfort, and community.
